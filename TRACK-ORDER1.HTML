Find out where your K-pop goodies are with just a few steps!

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Log into your Starry K-Shop account.
  2. Navigate to My Orders from the profile menu.
  3. Select the order you want to track.
  4. Click on Track Package to view real-time status.
